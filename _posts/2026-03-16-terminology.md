* BLOB (Binary Large Object) : store binary data like images, audio, or video files<br>
* CLOB (Character Large Object) : store large, character-based data such as long text, documents, or JSON/XML code.<br>
* DR (Disaster Recovery) strategy : ensures business continuity by minimizing downtime and data loss (RTO/RPO) during catastrophic failures<br>
